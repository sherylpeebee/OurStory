Let's be honest...

social media can be gross.

That's why I decided to make an app that could be used more selectively, and just with people who are on a need-to-know basis.

The high visibility virtual social scene definitely has its place. Is it cool that your great cousin thrice removed just had twins? It's the best! Do your fifth-grade Geography teacher's homemade tacos with free-range chicken look amazing? Without a doubt!

But after so long, it can feel like you're being bombarded and, dare I say, bludgeoned by the noise of people you sometimes can't even remeber how you met, clamoring for "likes".




I figured I'd sift through that a little.
